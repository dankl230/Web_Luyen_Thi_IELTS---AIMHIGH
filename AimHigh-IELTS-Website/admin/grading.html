Nguyễn Thảo — Writing Task 2

Nộp lúc: 10/03/2026 14:30 • Thời gian: 38 phút
Bài làm của học viên

Đề bài: Some people believe that technology has made our lives more complex, while others feel it has simplified things. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

In the modern era, technology has become an integral part of our daily lives. While some argue that technological advancements have complicated our existence, others contend that they have streamlined various aspects of life. In my opinion, while technology does introduce certain complexities, its overall impact has been overwhelmingly positive in simplifying our routines.

On the one hand, technology has undeniably added layers of complexity to our lives. The constant connectivity facilitated by smartphones and social media can lead to information overload, making it difficult for individuals to disconnect and relax. Furthermore, the rapid pace of technological change requires continuous learning and adaptation, which can be stressful for many people, particularly older generations who may struggle with new interfaces and systems.

On the other hand, the benefits of technology in simplifying daily tasks are immeasurable. From online banking and e-commerce to digital communication tools, technology has eliminated numerous manual processes that were once time-consuming and tedious...
